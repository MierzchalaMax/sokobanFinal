The rules of the game : The aim of this game is to push the rubies into the chests. These chests are located inside a room surrounded by walls. You control a character called "Sokoban" that can be traduced by "warehouse keeper" in Japanese. Press LEFT, RIGHT, UP and DOWN buttons to move the character. You cannot pass through walls and rubies, and you can only push one box at a time.
